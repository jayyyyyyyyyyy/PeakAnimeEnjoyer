# Hall Of Fame

## Goal

The Hall Of Fame keeps track of every anime watched by the club.

It becomes the permanent memory of the club.

---

# Status

Implemented.

The Hall Of Fame is calculated from revealed final reviews and finished seasons.

Mock rankings have been removed.

# Ranking Calculation

Every ranking is calculated from submitted reviews.

Only revealed seasons are included.

Each anime receives category averages across all revealed reviews for that season.

Ties are sorted by review count.
